Privacy Policy for Offside Call - Football Referee Simulator

Last updated: January 14, 2026

ilkemgamedesign ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Offside Call - Football Referee Simulator mobile application (the "App").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our App:

1. Anonymous Authentication

  • We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique identifier for each user
  • This allows you to save your progress and compete on leaderboards
  • No personal information (name, email, phone) is required or collected

2. Game Data

  • We store your game scores, progress, and nicknames on Firebase servers
  • This data is used solely for leaderboard functionality and game progress

3. Analytics

  • We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the App
  • This includes general gameplay patterns and crash reports
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

Data Storage

Your game data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages (Everyone/PEGI 3).

Data Deletion
